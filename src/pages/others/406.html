Privacy Policy of Omnicclipper

1. Introduction

At Omnicclipper ("we," "us," or "our"), we are dedicated to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our website and services. By using our services or visiting our website, you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy.

2. Scope of this Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all personal data we collect from you when you use our services or visit our website. It covers how we handle your data and ensures that your personal information is used responsibly.

3. Information We Collect

We collect both personal and non-personal information from you, which may include:
  • Personal Information: Name, email address, postal address, phone number, payment information, and other details necessary for our services.
  • Non-Personal Information: Device information, IP address, browser type, and other technical data.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:
  • To Provide Services: We use your data to deliver our image editing services to you, process your payments, and communicate with you about your orders.
  • To Improve Our Services: We analyze the information we collect to enhance the quality of our website and services.
  • For Marketing Purposes: With your consent, we may use your information to send you promotional materials about our services.

5. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information in the following ways:
  • With Our Team: Our employees and contractors may need access to your data to provide you with our services.
  • With Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our business, such as payment processors or web hosting services.
  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights or the rights of others.
We will never sell or rent your information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

6. Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these websites. We recommend that you review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

7. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to help us manage payments, send emails, and analyze website traffic. These services may collect and use your personal information according to their privacy policies.

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:
  • Access: You can request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Update: You can ask us to correct or update your personal information if it is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Delete: You can request that we delete your personal information.
  • Object: You can object to the processing of your data for specific purposes, such as marketing.
  • Restrict Processing: You can ask us to limit how we use your information.
To exercise these rights, please contact us at omnicclipper@gmail.com

9. Data Protection

Omnicclipper takes the security and confidentiality of your images seriously. We use secure servers and file transfer protocols to ensure your data remains protected at all times

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your information. If we make any significant changes, we will notify you by email or by posting a notice on our website.

11. Changes of Business Ownership and Control

If Omnicclipper is sold or merged with another company, your personal data may be transferred to the new owners.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us at omnicclipper@gmail.com
